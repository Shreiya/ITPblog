Prototyping My Life

Mobile Mummy

An Imperfect Prototype For The Perfect App

Remember all those times you'd be behind on, well basically, life?

But then you always had someone runnign after you with a slipper in hand, making sure you actually got stuff done? Yeah, my childhood was definitely better than yours. But adult life has become seemingly difficult. And that's why, I present to you

Mobile Mummy

The basic point of the app is for me to set timelines on the basic requirements for me to adult. But since I'm so used to having a chappal (slipper) follow me around, I need to survive in a state of constant fear to actually get shit done.

However, the reader needs to keep in mind that this is a prototype, and like all successful virtual products, the output needs to be completely different from what is originally planned.

So here we're going to have a regular login screen that reminds me what I'm getting myself into.
