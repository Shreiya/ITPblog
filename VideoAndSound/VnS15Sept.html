Learning

Creating the soundwalk was a collaboration between me and Alden, and it was possibly the first time I was sharing the creative space with another person. Usually, there's a clear division where one ideates and the other executes, but here both of us were at work.

Of course, it was pure luck that I was paired with someone who wanted the same theme as me. It just made the assignment even more fun than it was originally planned to be.

After review of course, I realised that while we focused a large part of our time in the ideating, we could have done a better job at the directions we were providing to the listener. On a completely personal note - I was fixated on the idea being as 'fun' as possible that I forgot that the listener needs to get to the venue to actually experience the heart of the walk.
