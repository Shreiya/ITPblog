An introduction

The importance of knowing a circuit.


Coming from a copywriting background, it's difficult to grasp the entire idea of anything 'scientific' when it's with reference to a GREAT idea. But that's why I'm here at ITP. To go back to high school, without actually going back to high school.

Anyway, what you see above is the most basic circuit one can manage. There's a wire connecting the positive current to the breadboard, which then gets connected to the anode of the LED. Then there's another wire connecting the cathode with the ground of the power source.

That's it. You're done.

And I just introduced you to the simplest 'voila' in the history of mankind.

To take it a step further, I decided to incorporate a button into this happy electronic mess.

Remember that LED I just showed you? I added a button to it that magically understood that it controlled the fate of the LED.

How is that anything less than magical?
